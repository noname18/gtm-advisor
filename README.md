# Dayforce GTM Advisor

An internal sales tool for Dayforce sales reps to quickly identify recommended product modules, Dayforce vs. partner guidance, and exceptions for any prospect industry.

## How to use
Go to: https://noname18.github.io/gtm-advisor

1. Enter the prospect's company name
2. Select their segment (Large Enterprise, Enterprise, Majors, Emerging, CA SMB)
3. Select geography (US, Canada, EMEA, APJ)
4. Select their primary vertical and sub-vertical
5. Click **Generate recommendations**

## What it shows
- Which product modules to sell
- Whether to lead with Dayforce or a partner
- Which specific partners are relevant (confirmed or not confirmed)
- Any segment exceptions or known product gaps

## Coverage
- North America data only (MVP)
- EMEA and APJ tabs coming in next version
